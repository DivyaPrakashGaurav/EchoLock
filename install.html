INSTALLATION GUIDE

Installation

01

Download

Download EchoLock from the official website.

02

Install

Run as Administrator and allow microphone permissions.

03

Calibrate

Record your unlock phrase multiple times.

How to Use

Set Your Voice Key

Enter a simple and clear voice keyword (for example: lock, Bye, Shutdown).
This keyword will be used to lock your PC using your voice.

Allow Microphone Access

Ensure your microphone is enabled and working properly.
EchoLock runs silently in the background and listens only for your voice key.

Manage & Check Commands

You can view, edit, or manage your voice commands by clicking on the Commands menu inside EchoLock.

Uninstallation

Open EchoLock

Launch EchoLock and navigate to Settings.

Start Uninstall Process

Click on the Continue Uninstall button.

Complete Uninstallation

Follow these steps to safely and completely remove EchoLock from your system.

01

Open Control Panel

Press Windows + R, type control, and press Enter. This will open the Windows Control Panel.

02

Uninstall EchoLock

Go to Programs > Programs and Features, locate EchoLock, and click Uninstall.

03

Restart System

Restart your PC to ensure all EchoLock services and background components are fully removed.
