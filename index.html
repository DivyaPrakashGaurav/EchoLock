Deployment

Setup Guide

01

Deployment

Download the EchoLock.zip. Extract and run the executable as Administrator to register the sound driver.

02

Calibration

The app will ask you to repeat your "Unlock Phrase" five times. This builds your unique acoustic profile.

03

Uninstallation

To remove, go to Settings > System Security inside the app and click "Disable Master Driver" before deleting files.
